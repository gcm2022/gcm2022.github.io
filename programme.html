Programme

Our (preliminary!) programme for the GCM'22 workshop can be found below. Rooms and streaming links are to be confirmed. Coffee breaks and lunch will be provided by the STAF organisers.

Note that the co-located ECMFA'22 keynote will take place the same day from 09:00–10:30.

    A Foundation for Functional Graph Programs - The Graph Transformation Control Algebra (GTA)

    A Graph-Transformational Approach for Proving the Correctness of Reductions between NP-Problems
